What to Include

Specific messages get faster answers.

01

Identify the product

Name the app or game, the platform, and the storefront you downloaded it from if relevant.

02

Describe the context

Include device model, operating system version, and the exact screen or feature where the issue or question occurs.

03

State the expected outcome

Tell us what you expected to happen and what happened instead. If this is a business inquiry, specify your proposal, market, and timeline.

Frequently Asked

Direct answers to common contact scenarios.

Where should users send a product issue or support request?

General support and user-facing product issues should be sent to support@ourappsx.com. That includes troubleshooting, policy questions, feature feedback, and non-enterprise billing context where app marketplace records are involved.

Which address should be used for commercial proposals?

Commercial proposals should be sent to bussiness@ourappsx.com unless the matter is explicitly strategic, founder-level, or enterprise-scale from the outset.

When should someone contact Junaidah directly?

Large account discussions, strategic alliances, custom studio engagements, IP opportunities, or decision-critical commercial proposals should be directed to Junaidah@ourappsx.com.

Do you provide technical and policy clarification for monetized apps?

Yes. The studio can address questions about product monetization models, official app marketplace payments, privacy handling, age-appropriate ad treatment, and the operational reasoning reflected in the published Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
